BRAND NAME:
Universal Laser Systems
DESCRIPTION:

TUMBLER HEAT PRESS

BRAND NAME:
PYD Life 2 in 1 Tumbler Press Machine
DESCRIPTION:
The tumbler press machine is 750 W, 110 V, with a temperature range of 0-482 F and time range of 0-999 S. It comes with two heat press attachments: one for the V3.0 30 oz heat press attachment, and another for new glass tumblers, cans, and kids water bottles. The 30 oz attachment can sublimate 11 or 15 oz ceramic mugs, while the slim attachment can sublimate 17 and 20 oz tumblers, 13 oz cans, 12 oz kids tumblers, and 17 oz cola shaped water bottle. It's easy to use, portable, and requires only a small work space.
