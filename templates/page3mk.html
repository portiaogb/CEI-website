EDIBLE PRINTER

BRAND NAME:
Eddie Edible Ink Printer
DESCRIPTION:
Eddie prints full-color photographs and text directly onto the surface of cookies, lollipops or other suitable food items. For example, the edible ink printer will hold up to twelve 3.5” (90mm) cookies on a circulating tray. Each cookie is automatically drawn into the printer one at a time. The printer then prints directly onto the surface of the item, rotates, and feeds and prints the next cookie.

MILLING MACHINE

BRAND NAME:
Levil LMV-F400 Milling Machine
DESCRIPTION:
The certification cart machine is a robust industrial CNC designed for 24/7 production of small parts in materials like brass, aluminum, and soft steel. It features a high-speed 17,000 RPM, 2HP liquid-cooled spindle, a large axis motion range, and an integrated coolant system. Equipped with a FANUC 0i-Mate MD control, it offers precise performance with resolutions as fine as 0.00004” (0.001mm) and rapid feeds up to 400 ipm (10,200 mm/min). The machine includes an eleven station automatic tool changer, capable of quick 8-second tool changes using standard S20T tool holders. It serves both as a reliable industrial workhorse and an effective educational tool for training on advanced CNC operations.
